About Injury Assistant

Injury Assistant is a sports injury advisor designed to help athletes, students, coaches and sports enthusiasts understand common sports injuries. The application provides information on causes, prevention methods, and recovery recommendations based on the selected sport, injury type, and pain level.

How It Works

Select your sport, choose the injury type, and indicate your pain level. The system then generates tailored guidance to help you understand the injury and make informed decisions about recovery.
